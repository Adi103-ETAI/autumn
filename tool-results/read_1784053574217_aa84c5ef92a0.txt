   267â†’## 3. Multiplayer merge logic (`applyRemoteCanvas` â€” `:20473`)
   268â†’
   269â†’Called when a LiveBlocks/peer update arrives. It is **MERGE-PRESERVE**, not a wholesale
   270â†’replace: transient/per-machine fields must not be clobbered.
   271â†’
   272â†’1. **Build local lookup maps** (screen/chat/term/browser/remotion by id) and compute
   273â†’   `liveTermIds` (terminals whose `terminalHealth` shows not-exited) and `liveChatIds`
   274â†’   (chats with `busyTaskId` or `streamingByChat`).
   275â†’2. **Drop-guard** (`guardRemoteNodeDrop` `:19379`): if a remote snapshot omits a live local
   276â†’   terminal/chat, keep the local one (prevents a transient peer disconnect from killing a
   277â†’   running agent). Tracks `"remote_drop_guarded"` events.
   278â†’3. **Compute removed ids** (local nodes absent from incoming) â†’ `teardownRemovedNode()`
   279â†’   (kills PTY / unregisters bus node).
   280â†’4. **Per-kind merge preserving local-only fields:**
   281â†’   - screens: keep `max(local.screenVersion, remote.screenVersion)` (never regress preview) + `previewMode`/`worktreePath` `:20546`.
   282â†’   - chatBoxes: keep `messages`, `busyTaskId`, `harness`, `model`/`effort`/`permission`, `worktreePath`, `branch`, `baseRepoPath` from local; drop `:20559`.
   283â†’   - youtube: keep `extractedMarkdown` (cached snapshot) `:20580`.
   284â†’   - remotion: keep `folder` `:20585`.
   285â†’   - terminals: keep `cwd`, `harness`, `startCommand`, `worktreePath`, `branch`, `baseRepoPath` `:20590`.
   286â†’   - **Peer-added terminals are gated**: appended to `remoteAddedTerminals[]` so they don't
   287â†’     auto-boot a PTY until the local user approves on the canvas node (`FullScreenChat`
   288â†’     shows "Added by a collaborator â€” approve it" `FullScreenChat:837`).
